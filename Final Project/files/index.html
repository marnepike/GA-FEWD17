Become a Member Owner

Our member owners are the heart of our cooperative! There are a variety of membership options. All membership options entitle customers to store discounts and to run for board membership. As of February 1st, 2015, our annual membership fee is $10.00. Choose the membership option best suited to your lifestyle!

How to Become a Member

Signing up is easy! Submit the membership form above, then simply stop by the co-op with your deposit and tell any staff member that you've submitted your membership form online. We'll provide you with a membership card that you can show when you shop at the co-op to get your discount. Don't trust the internet? Come on in and tell us you want to sign up - we'll get you started with good ol' pen and paper!

Standard Membership

All member owners receive a 5% discount off of the shelf price on every item purchased.

  • 5% discount off of the shelf price
  • No volunteer work hours required

Working Membership

Volunteers are a very important part of the Co-op. It’s fun and easy to participate! Jobs like restocking the shelves, pricing items and cleaning keep the Co-op running smoothly and looking great.

  • 10% discount off of the shelf price
  • 8 hours of volunteer work required (2 hours every 3 months)

Senior Membership

Volunteers are a very important part of the Co-op. It’s fun and easy to participate! Jobs like restocking the shelves, pricing items and cleaning keep the Co-op running smoothly and looking great.

  • 10% discount off of the shelf price
  • All people age 62 and over are eligible
  • No volunteer work hours required
